Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my fav foods.Here is a reciepe for delicious rich scambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has been melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan te centre every 20seconds(as if you are making an omlete)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on plate until served)
